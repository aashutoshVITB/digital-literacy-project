## Project Overview

This repository contains my **Digital Literacy Portfolio Project**, completed as part of the **CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course**.  
In this project, I worked as a **Student Digital Ambassador**, focusing on helping peers understand digital tools, professional online communication, and safe internet practices.

The project consists of **five tasks**, each covering an important aspect of digital literacy. These tasks include creating an infographic, building a digital portfolio, exploring collaboration platforms, practicing professional email etiquette, and researching cybercrime awareness.

Through this project, I learned how digital tools can be used effectively for academic collaboration, communication, and personal development while maintaining responsible and safe online behavior.

## Task Summary

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a visual infographic explaining digital literacy concepts using **Canva**. The infographic highlights key topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and the importance of digital literacy.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on platforms such as **GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle** to begin building a digital portfolio that can showcase skills, projects, and professional growth over the next four years.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner-level coding challenge on **HackerRank** and created a **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms**. The responses were connected to **Google Sheets** for easy data collection and analysis.

**Google Form Link:**  
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeXTNMK-pEDU2dedH4aydjdL8BOD8Yk6KsWQwybpKBDAhJB6A/viewform?usp=publish-editor

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Digital Communication
Drafted two professional emails:
- Requesting an assignment extension from a professor
- Expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity

Also created a **Social Media Do’s and Don’ts checklist** to promote responsible digital behavior among college students.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched **UPI/Online Payment Fraud** and created a case study explaining how such scams occur, who is targeted, and their consequences.  
Additionally, I prepared a **"Stay Safe Online" prevention checklist** with practical cybersecurity tips for students.
