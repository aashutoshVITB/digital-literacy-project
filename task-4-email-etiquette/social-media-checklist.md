# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

As a college student and digital ambassador, it is important to use social media responsibly and professionally. The following checklist highlights good practices for safe and respectful online behavior.

## Do's

- **Think before you post** – Ensure your content is respectful, appropriate, and does not harm others.
- **Protect your privacy** – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings on your social media accounts.
- **Verify information before sharing** – Make sure news or information is from a reliable source.
- **Maintain a professional online presence** – Remember that future employers or professors may see your profiles.
- **Respect others online** – Communicate politely and be mindful of different opinions.

## Don'ts

- **Do not share personal or sensitive information** such as passwords, phone numbers, or home addresses.
- **Do not post offensive or inappropriate content** that could damage your reputation.
- **Do not spread fake news or misinformation** on social media platforms.
- **Do not engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or online arguments.**
- **Do not overshare personal details** that may compromise your safety or privacy.
